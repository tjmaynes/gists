Chu and Blossom.

Cinema.

I've loved movies since I could rewind 'Back to the Future' on a VCR (around age 4). It wasn't until I started skating and getting into skate videos did I start thinking about doing working in that 'industry' for the rest of my life. With the help of some insightful movies, candid people, and Stanley Kubrick, I began my life long adventure in filmmaking at the age of 13. I believe that to "know" film one must know "photography" and for what can be done with the extent of one's own creative imagination is to inspire others to do the same. I couldn't be happier.
